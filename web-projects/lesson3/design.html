Color Scheme

For the color scheme, I decided to go with a range of colors that are similar to teal, greay and a tan color.

For the background, I wanted to use a tan color. The headers would be a dark grey. And the headers are a teal color.

Fonts

Titles and header fonts would be the DM Serif Display because they have a darker bold to it.

The paragraphs and footer would be the Bree Serif font.
