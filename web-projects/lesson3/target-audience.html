Target Audience

The target audience for my chamber of commerce website is anybody that is trying to get to know their local city better.

Personas

Persona 1

This person loves to go on adventures with their friends and family to find the next best food and desserts. But most of all to spend time with their friends and family.

Persona 2

This person likes to go on walks on the neighboring trails. They also like to go shopping, eat great food, and watch movies.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

Where is the best new foods to try? This website would give a list of suggestions of where a person may go for restaurants. It would be categorized by ethnicity. This site would also give suggestions on places where a person could go for desserts and treats.

Scenario 2

Where are some places a person can go to walk on trails? A person can come here to find out where the best local trails that they can go on and where it would take them.

Scenario 3

Where is a great place to go shopping and hangout with friends? This website would list the local shopping malls and have give suggestions of what they could do there. Example: shopping, food, desserts, etc.

Scenario 4

What is there to do that is family friendly? There would be listings of different outdoor parks, theme parks, and other places.

Scenario 5

When is the best time to plan an event outside and where? With this website, it gives a general idea of what the weather is like. It also gives suggestions of places where you could hold an event.
